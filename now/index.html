Now

Say what you’d tell a friend you hadn’t seen in a year.

For the last 3 years I’ve been woolgathering thoughts for a new essay on leisure, idleness, lived time, and the attention economy. I’m currently writing the rough draft—and I’m very excited about this project.

I wasn’t working for the 160 days between October and March. It was a profoundly important period because it provided some much needed space to reflect on life and otherwise do things that i didn’t have time for. It was also stressful since the job market was very competitive.

I recently started a new role as Software Engineer at a cybersecurity company .
